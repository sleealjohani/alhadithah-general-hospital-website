# Content Guide

## Tone

- Official, clear, and calm.
- Arabic is primary. English should be a clean equivalent, not a word-by-word weak translation.
- Avoid marketing claims.

## Do Not Publish

- Fake phone numbers or emails.
- Bed counts, visitor counts, satisfaction rates, or other statistics unless approved.
- Patient information.
- Personal staff data.
- Random stock photos.
- Unapproved official links.

## Recommended Content Flow

1. Add services and departments first.
2. Add knowledge center categories.
3. Add approved forms and links.
4. Add public contact details only after official confirmation.
5. Publish news only after internal approval.
6. Add doctor profiles only with the staff member's consent and an approved photo.
7. Build standalone pages (e.g. a Patient Guide) via **Admin → Pages**, then link them from **Admin → Navigation** once approved.

## Images and Files

Use only:

- Official hospital images.
- Approved cluster or hospital logos.
- Approved initiative media.

If no official image exists, keep the clean gradient placeholder instead of adding random stock imagery.

All uploads go through **Admin → Media Library**, which stores files in
Supabase Storage. Use the public bucket only for content that is genuinely
safe for anyone to access directly by URL; use the private (hospital
documents) bucket for anything internal.
